# Prompt Review Checklist

Before using any prompt:

* Evidence is included.
* Risk level is included.
* Expected benefit is included.
* Rollback plan is included.
* Manual approval is required.
* Product material is verified.
* Product dimensions are verified or omitted.
* No medical, healing, luck, religious-power, or guarantee claims.
* No automatic Etsy upload.
* Constitution compliance is recorded in the Growth Journal.
