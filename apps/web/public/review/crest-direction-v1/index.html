Classic Shield Tree Crest Direction v1

A reset from procedural line-art variation toward high-fidelity, reference-quality black and antique gold crest concepts. This gallery is for Founder and CSO review only and is not integrated into production order delivery.

Do not link this page from public navigation. These are concept images, not customer artifacts, official coats of arms, legal heraldic grants, noble title claims, or certified genealogical records.
Theme Continuity and unity, expressed through a shield, tree of life, and woven root/knot structure.
Visual Standard Embossed antique gold, dark archival ground, realistic depth, private heirloom mood.
Decision Needed Choose one primary direction, one secondary fallback, and one rejected boundary direction.

01 Classic Shield Tree

balancedornate shieldtree + knot

Rationale

Baseline concept for the new direction: strong shield, tree roots, unity knot, and blank ribbon without model-generated text.

Continuity + Unity

The tree carries continuity; the knot under the roots turns family connection into the visual anchor.

Prompt used
Concept 01, Classic Shield Tree Crest. A luxury black and antique gold family legacy crest, ornate shield frame, central tree of life with visible roots, a subtle Celtic knot integrated near the roots, side laurel branches, bottom ribbon/plaque with NO TEXT, embossed raised metallic gold relief, dark archival leather background, dramatic soft lighting, centered symmetrical composition, high-end keepsake product.
