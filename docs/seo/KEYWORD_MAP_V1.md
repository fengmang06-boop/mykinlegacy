## Cannibalization controls

- Home owns broad `personalized family gift` intent.
- Collection owns the product and `custom family crest gift` conversion intent.
- `/gifts/...` owns specific buyer/occasion intent.
- Example details prove fit and should not compete with their parent gift page for the broad keyword.
- Informational articles must answer a question first and link to, rather than duplicate, commercial landing pages.
- The legacy AI-generator URL is excluded from the sitemap and must not become a competing commercial page.
