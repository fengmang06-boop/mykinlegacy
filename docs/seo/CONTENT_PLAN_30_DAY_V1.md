# MyKinLegacy 30-Day SEO Content Plan V1

The plan publishes no more than five new articles in the first 30 days. Remaining work improves, measures, and internally links the core commercial cluster. This protects quality and Founder Edition delivery capacity.

## Month-one success criteria

- Five high-quality articles or fewer; no thin or scaled publishing.
- All five add useful information even if the reader does not purchase.
- No unsupported heraldry, genealogy, noble, royal, or family-history claims.
- Search Console shows the sitemap fetched successfully.
- At least the core pages become discovered or indexed.
- Organic landing visits can be separated from direct/internal visits in the chosen measurement system.
- No increase in checkout, Founder review, email, Vault, or delivery incidents.
