# FAQ and Schema Recommendation

## Visible FAQ

Eight visible questions address meaning, parents who have everything, sibling coordination, milestone materials, adult-child perspective, deeper personalization, digital suitability, and timing.

## Structured Data

- `Article`: recommended.
- `BreadcrumbList`: `Home > Journal > Article title`.
- `FAQPage`: not recommended by default; only consider if all visible FAQ content remains unchanged and current search policy makes it eligible.
- Do not add `Product` or `Review` markup to an editorial article.
- Do not claim ratings, inventory, author credentials, or delivery dates not present on the page.

## QA

- H1 and meta intent aligned: PASS.
- FAQ is visible and substantive: PASS.
- Anniversary convention statements are qualified by culture/source: PASS.
- Digital delivery disclosure appears before conversion: PASS.
