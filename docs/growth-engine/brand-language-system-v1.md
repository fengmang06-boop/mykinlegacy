# Brand Language System v1.0

Status: Growth Engine Sprint 1  
Brand: MyKinLegacy  
Purpose: Define the language system that makes MyKinLegacy feel human, trusted, collectible, and meaning-first.

MyKinLegacy should never sound like a generic AI generator. It should sound like a careful guide helping a family preserve meaning.

## 1. Language Principle

Lead with meaning.

Support with trust.

Mention technology only when necessary.

The customer should feel:

"This understands why my family matters."

Not:

"This generated some files."

## 2. Preferred Vocabulary

Use:

- Collection
- Artifacts
- Journey
- Discover
- Legacy
- Identity
- Family Story
- Meaning
- Vault
- Preserve
- Honor
- Remember
- Receive
- Guided Interview
- Symbolic
- Heritage-inspired
- Private by default
- Family values
- Keepsake
- Recognition

## 3. Forbidden Vocabulary

Avoid in customer-facing value language:

- Files
- Download
- Generate
- Package
- Prompt
- AI result
- Output bundle
- Image dump
- Official crest
- Certified heraldry
- Real ancestral arms
- Prove your ancestry
- Royal bloodline
- Logo
- Template
- Clipart

Operational terms may exist internally. Brand language must not center them.

## 4. Replacement Rules

| Avoid | Use |
| --- | --- |
| Files | Artifacts |
| Download | Receive your Collection |
| Generate | Create / Prepare / Interpret |
| Package | Collection |
| AI result | AI-assisted symbolic artifact |
| Form | Guided Interview |
| Order status | Collection progress |
| Download page | Collection Vault |
| Image variants | Crest artifacts |
| PDF files | Story artifacts |

## 5. CTA Rules

Primary CTAs:

- Begin Your Collection
- Create My Family Legacy
- Start the Guided Interview
- Receive Your Collection
- Preserve This Story

Secondary CTAs:

- Explore the Collection
- Discover Your Family Symbols
- See What Is Included
- Learn How It Works
- Review the Disclaimer

Never use:

- Generate now
- Download files
- Get AI images
- Claim official crest

## 6. Trust Language

Required trust phrases:

- personalized, AI-assisted, heritage-inspired symbolic design
- not official, legally granted, or historically certified
- not genealogical proof
- private by default
- created from your inputs

Trust language should appear naturally, not as hidden fine print.

## 7. AI Wording

Use:

- AI-assisted
- AI-supported creative process
- interpreted before visual creation
- guided by your family inputs

Avoid:

- AI knows your family
- AI proves your ancestry
- AI creates your identity
- AI official heraldry

AI is a tool. Meaning is the hero.

## 8. Identity Language

Use language that includes:

- inherited family
- chosen family
- blended family
- rebuilt family
- remembered family

Avoid bloodline-only language.

Family identity is not only surname, country, or ancestry. It is values, story, memory, and belonging.

## 9. Gift Language

A meaningful gift should be framed as:

- something that says what words often cannot
- a way to honor someone
- a keepsake worth preserving
- a story made visible
- a gift that notices who they are

Avoid framing gifts as:

- cheap
- instant
- novelty
- AI-made surprise

## 10. Support Tone

Support should be:

- calm
- human
- precise
- non-defensive
- emotionally aware

Preferred phrases:

- I can help you locate your Collection Vault.
- We can review whether the collection reflects the intended meaning.
- Because this is a personalized digital collection, refund eligibility depends on generation and delivery state.
- We cannot create official arms, but we can help shape a symbolic alternative.

## 12. Strategic Conclusion

MyKinLegacy language must make one thing clear:

We do not sell files.

We do not sell prompts.

We do not sell AI.

We help families turn meaning into artifacts worth keeping.
