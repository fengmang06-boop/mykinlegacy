# Product Universe v1.0

Status: Growth Engine Sprint 1  
Brand: MyKinLegacy  
Purpose: Map 50+ future Collections without authorizing immediate product expansion.

This is a universe map, not a build plan. No product should be executed before first-sales validation.

## 1. Product Universe Principle

Every future Collection must answer:

What human transformation happens?

If a Collection does not help a family preserve, express, gift, recognize, or pass down meaning, it does not belong.

- Family Legacy Collection
- Father's/Parents gift wedge
- Wedding test
- Motto/values/certificate entry offers
- Wall art validation

### Year 2

Focus:

- Wedding expansion
- parents/mother/grandparent gifts
- story mini products
- reunion and anniversary collections

### Year 3

Focus:

- memory products
- books
- archive starter
- family time capsule
- sensitive categories with review

### Year 4

Focus:

- subscription validation
- vault membership
- physical extensions
- adoption-sensitive and deeper family identity products

### Year 5

Focus:

- multi-generation family identity platform
- family archive ecosystem
- physical + digital continuity
- repeat legacy updates

## 4. Strategic Rule

The universe can be large.

Execution must be narrow.

The first 100 sales should focus on one or two wedges only.
