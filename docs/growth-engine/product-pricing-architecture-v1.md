# Product & Pricing Architecture v1.0

Status: Growth Engine Sprint 1  
Brand: MyKinLegacy  
Purpose: Define the commercial ladder from free trust-building to premium, subscription, and physical revenue.

MyKinLegacy does not sell AI output. It sells meaning made tangible: a private, symbolic family identity collection that helps a family recognize, gift, share, and preserve what matters.

## 1. Commercial Thesis

Customers pay when MyKinLegacy turns emotional ambiguity into a meaningful family artifact.

They pay because:

- a gift needs emotional weight
- a family story feels fragile
- a milestone needs ritual
- identity needs expression
- memory needs preservation
- future generations need something to inherit

The business should price transformation, not file count.

## 2. Pricing Ladder

```text
Free
  -> Entry
    -> Core
      -> Premium
        -> Subscription
          -> Physical
```

| Level | Role | Price posture | Goal |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free | Trust and discovery | $0 | Help users clarify meaning |
| Entry | First paid commitment | $9-$39 | Convert light intent |
| Core | Main revenue product | $39-$99 | Deliver full collection value |
| Premium | Deeper emotional use case | $99-$299 | Serve wedding, memory, memorial, parent gifts |
| Subscription | Ongoing family archive | $49-$149/year | Preserve and update legacy over time |
| Physical | Tangible heirloom extension | $39-$499+ | Convert digital meaning into display or gift objects |

## 3. Free Layer

Free should not give away the complete collection.

Free should help users answer:

- What does my family stand for?
- What motto fits us?
- What symbol carries our values?
- What gift moment matters right now?
- What story should we preserve?

Free opportunities:

- Family Motto Generator
- Family Values Builder
- Family Symbol Finder
- Heritage Gift Finder
- Guardian Animal Finder
- Legacy Letter Prompt
- Family Story Starter

Free creates trust and better inputs.

## 4. Entry Layer

Entry products should be small, meaningful, and giftable.

Examples:

| Entry offer | Human need | Price range |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Family Motto Pack | Find words for family identity | $9-$19 |
| Symbol Meaning Pack | Understand symbols before committing | $9-$29 |
| Heritage Certificate Pack | Create a printable gift artifact | $19-$39 |
| Legacy Letter Pack | Say what is hard to say | $19-$39 |
| Family Story Starter | Begin preserving memory | $19-$49 |

Entry products are not the destination. They are trust bridges.

## 5. Core Product

Core product:

Family Legacy Collection.

Core transformation:

Scattered family inputs -> private symbolic family identity.

Target price:

- Launch learning range: $39-$69
- Mature core range: $69-$99

The core product must feel complete. It should not feel like an upsell trap.

## 6. Premium Layer

Premium is justified by emotional depth, ceremony, sensitivity, or expanded interpretation.

| Premium offer | Why premium |
| --- | --- |
| Wedding Heritage Collection | Event identity, ceremony use, visual importance |
| Father's Legacy Gift | Emotional gift, recipient specificity |
| Grandparent Memory Collection | Memory preservation, story depth |
| Memorial Legacy Collection | Sensitive handling and remembrance value |
| Family Story Book | Long-form keepsake |
| Family Reunion Kit | Multi-person sharing and event identity |

Premium must not mean more files. It must mean deeper meaning.

## 7. Subscription Layer

Subscription should only launch after repeat value is proven.

Future subscription:

MyKinLegacy Vault.

Subscription value must include:

- preserved collections
- annual legacy updates
- family memory prompts
- children/grandchildren versions
- event reminders
- private family archive
- shared family access controls

Do not sell storage alone. Sell continuity.

## 8. Physical Layer

Physical products should follow validated digital demand.

Possible physical extensions:

- framed legacy print
- printed certificate set
- family story book
- wedding stationery
- wax seal
- signet ring preview or partner product
- archival gift box

Physical products should make meaning more permanent.

## 9. Upgrade Logic

Customers upgrade when:

- the recipient matters more
- the moment is more ceremonial
- the story is deeper
- the family wants to preserve or display
- another family member wants their own version
- the collection becomes part of a recurring family archive

Upgrade language:

- Complete the story
- Preserve it in the Vault
- Create a gift-ready version
- Add a printed keepsake
- Continue the legacy

Avoid:

- unlock your files
- buy more credits
- generate more outputs

## 10. Repeat Purchase Logic

Customers return because life changes:

- wedding
- first child
- Father's Day
- Mother's Day
- anniversary
- grandparent aging
- memorial
- family reunion
- child asks about roots
- new home

Repeat purchase is life-moment driven, not feature driven.

## 11. Strategic Conclusion

MyKinLegacy's commercial model should grow from meaning:

Free helps people discover meaning.

Entry helps them test meaning.

Core turns meaning into a collection.

Premium deepens meaning for major life moments.

Subscription preserves meaning over time.

Physical makes meaning tangible.
