# MyKinLegacy Product Universe v1.0

Status: Five-year product strategy  
Primary brand: MyKinLegacy  
Product anchor: Family Legacy Collection  
Principle: Every product must extend family identity, memory, legacy, or meaningful gifting.

This document maps the future MyKinLegacy product universe. It is a strategic planning document, not an implementation request. No product in this universe should be built until the core Family Legacy Collection is trusted, stable, and commercially validated.

## 1. Product Universe Principles

MyKinLegacy products must:

- Solve a human demand before a search query.
- Feel like collections or meaningful artifacts, not random digital files.
- Preserve the symbolic, heritage-inspired, non-official trust boundary.
- Protect privacy by default.
- Expand from the Family Legacy Collection instead of fragmenting the brand.
- Support future digital, physical, and subscription revenue without becoming generic.

Difficulty scale:

- Low: mostly packaging, copy, or lighter artifact variation.
- Medium: new templates, richer prompts, or additional deliverables.
- High: sensitive handling, multi-asset workflows, physical operations, collaboration, or subscription systems.

Strategic priority:

- 1: Near-term high priority
- 2: Strong follow-up opportunity
- 3: Long-term moat or seasonal expansion
- 4: Future optional expansion

## 3. Category Strategy

### Identity

Identity products define "who we are." They should remain closest to the core Family Legacy Collection and serve as the foundation for all other categories.

Priority products:

- Family Legacy Collection
- Symbolic Family Crest Collection
- Family Motto Pack
- Family Values Pack
- House Name Collection

### Gift

Gift products convert fastest when the recipient and occasion are clear. They should package the same core value in gift-ready language.

Priority products:

- Family Legacy Gift
- Father's Legacy Gift
- Parents Heritage Gift
- Christmas Family Keepsake

### Wedding

Wedding products are visual, high-intent, and Pinterest-friendly. They require stronger style control and optional event-specific assets.

Priority products:

- Wedding Heritage Collection
- Wedding Crest Idea Pack
- Wedding Stationery Symbol Pack

### Memory

Memory products have high emotional intensity and higher trust risk. They should be developed carefully with sensitive language.

Priority products:

- Grandparent Memory Collection
- Family Story Starter Pack
- Parent Interview Collection
- Memorial Legacy Collection

### Children

Children products translate family identity into future-facing keepsakes. They should be gentle, clear, and parent-friendly.

Priority products:

- Baby Legacy Welcome Pack
- Child Family Storybook
- Family Values for Kids Pack

### Parents and Grandparents

These products are strong because they map to high-emotion gift moments.

Priority products:

- Father's Legacy Collection
- Mother's Story Collection
- Grandparent Interview Kit
- Grandparent Legacy Book

### Legacy

Legacy products deepen the brand into long-term family meaning.

Priority products:

- Family Time Capsule
- Annual Legacy Review
- Family Values Certificate
- Legacy Letter products

### Heritage

Heritage products require careful cultural and disclaimer handling.

Priority products:

- Immigrant Heritage Story Pack
- Blended Family Identity Pack
- Heritage Gift Collection

### Art and Wall Art

Art products improve average order value and giftability. They should derive from validated digital artifacts.

Priority products:

- Legacy Wall Art
- Family Motto Wall Art
- Heritage Poster Collection
- Family Reunion Banner Kit

### Books

Books are deep premium products. They require more story quality, structure, and likely future print workflows.

Priority products:

- Family Story Book Mini
- Family Legacy Book
- Grandparent Legacy Book

### Certificates

Certificates are strong add-ons but must not imply official certification.

Priority products:

- Heritage Certificate Pack
- Family Values Certificate
- Memorial Certificate Pack

### Vault and Subscription

Vault products should wait until repeat value is meaningful. They should not be launched as empty storage.

Priority products:

- Private Family Archive Starter
- Collection Vault Membership
- Annual Legacy Review

### Physical Products

Physical products should follow digital validation.

Priority products:

- Framed Legacy Print
- Wax Seal Design Collection
- Signet Ring Preview Collection

## 4. Product Evolution Roadmap

### Year 1

Objective: Validate the core commercial engine.

Build and improve:

- Family Legacy Collection
- Symbolic Family Crest Collection
- Heritage Gift Collection
- Family Legacy Gift
- Father's Legacy Gift
- Wedding Crest Idea Pack
- Family Motto Pack
- Family Symbol Finder as a free-to-paid bridge
- Heritage Certificate Pack
- Legacy Wall Art

Avoid in Year 1:

- Subscription
- Complex physical fulfillment
- Multi-member family archive
- Mass surname products
- Sensitive memorial automation without review standards

### Year 2

Objective: Expand into high-intent life moments.

Add:

- Wedding Heritage Collection
- Parents Heritage Gift
- Grandparent Interview Kit
- Grandparent Memory Collection
- Family Story Starter Pack
- Anniversary Legacy Gift
- Christmas Family Keepsake
- Family Story Book Mini
- Family Reunion Banner Kit
- Family Social Kit

Operational focus:

- Better examples
- Better gift positioning
- Better regeneration/review workflows
- Add-on attach rates

### Year 3

Objective: Deepen memory, books, and early vault behavior.

Add:

- Family Legacy Book
- Grandparent Legacy Book
- Parent Interview Collection
- Memorial Legacy Collection with careful review
- Private Family Archive Starter
- Family Time Capsule
- Immigrant Heritage Story Pack
- Blended Family Identity Pack
- Heritage Poster Collection
- Wax Seal Design Collection

Operational focus:

- Quality review
- Sensitive content handling
- Longer-form story quality
- Early repeat purchase loops

### Year 5

Objective: Become a digital family identity platform.

Potential expansions:

- Collection Vault Membership
- Annual Legacy Review
- Multi-member family collaboration
- Secure family sharing controls
- Physical framed prints
- Premium print books
- Signet ring preview and partner fulfillment
- Wedding stationery extensions
- Multilingual family story products
- Family archive and life-event timeline

Year 5 goal:

MyKinLegacy should be known not as a generator, but as the trusted place to create, preserve, revisit, and gift personalized family legacy collections.

## 5. Strategic Conclusion

The MyKinLegacy product universe should grow like a tree, not a shelf.

The root is Family Legacy Collection. The branches are life moments: gift, wedding, memory, children, parents, grandparents, legacy, heritage, art, books, certificates, vault, physical products, and subscription.

The test for every future product is simple:

Does this help a family preserve, express, or share its identity with dignity and trust?

If yes, it can belong. If no, it should not enter the universe.
