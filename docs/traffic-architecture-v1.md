# MyKinLegacy Traffic Architecture v1.0

Status: Three-year traffic system design  
Project: G009  
Primary question: How should all traffic channels work together over the next three years?  
Scope: Owned, earned, shared, search, AI, community, email, referral, and direct traffic architecture.

This document does not create pages, write SEO content, build tools, develop features, or define implementation tasks.

Traffic should not be treated as isolated channels.

Traffic should become a compounding system.

## 1. Traffic Philosophy

MyKinLegacy should not chase traffic for its own sake.

The brand needs qualified family-intent traffic:

- people looking for meaningful gifts
- people trying to preserve family stories
- couples planning symbolic wedding identity
- children honoring parents or grandparents
- families exploring values, symbols, and legacy
- people asking AI for thoughtful family keepsake ideas

The traffic philosophy:

1. Human demand comes before channel demand.
2. Every channel should reinforce the same trust language.
3. Search should capture intent.
4. Pinterest should create imagination.
5. AI Search should recommend frameworks and trusted explanations.
6. Reddit should reveal objections, not be treated as a sales floor.
7. Email should deepen relationship after permission.
8. Referral should come from family pride, not forced incentives.
9. Direct traffic should grow as brand memory compounds.

The highest-quality traffic is not the largest traffic.

It is traffic that arrives with a life moment.

## 2. Traffic Pyramid

The traffic pyramid defines how channels support each other.

```text
          Direct Brand
              ^
          Referral / Sharing
              ^
       AI Search / Earned Mentions
              ^
       Google Search / Pinterest
              ^
        Owned Trust Foundations
              ^
        Human Demand Research
```

### Traffic Layers

| Layer | Definition | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Owned | Brand site, trust pages, collection pages, guides, tools, email list | Foundation of authority and conversion |
| Earned | Mentions, citations, customer stories, AI references, organic discussions | Trust multiplier |
| Shared | Family group chats, Pinterest saves, Facebook shares, wedding/reunion sharing | Human network distribution |
| Search | Google, Bing, YouTube search behavior | Intent capture |
| AI | ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Copilot | Advisory recommendation layer |
| Community | Reddit, Facebook groups, wedding groups, genealogy communities | Feedback, objections, and life-moment discovery |

### Relationship Between Layers

Owned content gives channels something trustworthy to point to.

Search finds the owned content.

AI cites or summarizes the owned content.

Pinterest visualizes the owned content.

Community tests whether the owned content feels believable.

Shared traffic spreads through family networks.

Direct traffic grows when the brand becomes memorable.

## 3. Google Architecture

Google should capture explicit search intent across the customer journey.

### Page Type Relationships

```text
Commercial Pages
  -> Collection Pages
  -> Gift / Wedding / Meaning Pages
  -> Guides
  -> Tools
  -> Library
  -> FAQ / Trust Pages
```

### Architecture Layers

| Layer | Purpose | Example intent |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Commercial pages | Convert purchase-ready users | Family Legacy Collection, Heritage Gift |
| Collection pages | Explain specific offer | Family Legacy Collection, Wedding Heritage Collection |
| Tool pages | Capture active self-discovery | Family Motto Generator, Symbol Finder |
| Guide pages | Educate and build trust | How to create a family legacy |
| Library pages | Build long-term meaning authority | Animal symbolism, color meaning |
| FAQ pages | Remove objections | Is this official heraldry? Is it private? |
| Meaning pages | Connect symbols to human context | What does oak mean in family symbolism? |
| Gift pages | Capture emotional purchase intent | Meaningful gift for dad |
| Wedding pages | Capture event and visual intent | Wedding crest ideas |
| Collection support pages | Explain delivery and trust | Digital delivery, refund, disclaimer |

### Google Flow

User searches:

"meaningful gift for dad who has everything"

They land on a gift page.

The gift page explains the human problem.

It links to Family Legacy Collection or Father's Legacy Gift.

Trust pages clarify symbolic, non-official positioning.

The user starts the interview or saves the idea.

### Google Priorities

Year 1:

- gift intent
- Family Legacy Collection
- symbolic family crest
- wedding crest ideas
- family motto
- trust/disclaimer education

Year 2:

- memory and grandparent story
- anniversary and reunion
- symbol library
- deeper family story guides

Year 3:

- family archive
- vault
- children legacy
- multi-generation content

## 4. AI Search Architecture

AI Search is not classic SEO.

AI systems recommend sources that are clear, structured, trusted, and safe to summarize.

### AI Platforms

| Platform | Likely user behavior | MyKinLegacy opportunity |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ChatGPT | Advice, gift ideas, explanation, decision support | Become a recommended meaningful family gift/category |
| Gemini | Search-assisted planning and summaries | Be visible through structured, crawlable explainers |
| Claude | Reflective family questions and writing help | Own nuanced legacy and family identity frameworks |
| Perplexity | Citation-seeking research | Provide citation-ready definitions and comparisons |
| Copilot | Search-based recommendations | Appear in gift, family history, and keepsake answers |

### What AI Search Should Naturally Cite

AI systems are more likely to reference:

- clear definitions
- neutral explainers
- comparison pages
- FAQ structures
- safe disclaimers
- original frameworks
- practical checklists
- non-hype language

### AI Search Target Questions

MyKinLegacy should be structurally visible for questions like:

- What is a meaningful gift for my father?
- What should I give parents who have everything?
- How can I preserve my family story?
- How do I create a family legacy?
- What is a symbolic family crest?
- What is the difference between a coat of arms and a symbolic family design?
- How can AI help create a family keepsake?
- How do I make a wedding crest meaningful?

### AI Search Architecture

```text
Clear Definition
  -> Original Framework
  -> Safe Disclaimer
  -> Practical Use Case
  -> Collection Example
  -> Trust Page
```

AI Search will not be won by keyword stuffing.

It will be won by being the safest and clearest answer.

## 6. Reddit Architecture

Reddit is for listening, objection discovery, and careful participation.

It is not a direct sales channel.

### Suitable Reddit Needs

| Need | Why suitable |
| --- | --- |
| Family history uncertainty | Users discuss roots, records, and identity |
| Genealogy frustration | Good place to understand objections |
| Meaningful gift ideas | Users ask for honest suggestions |
| Wedding crest skepticism | Useful for trust language testing |
| AI product skepticism | Useful for positioning feedback |
| Memorial keepsake sensitivity | Useful only with care |
| Adoption/blended family language | Good for learning sensitivity |

### What Not To Do on Reddit

Never:

- spam product links
- pretend to be a customer
- astroturf
- push grief products
- argue with heraldry experts
- claim official crest authority
- post low-effort AI images
- ask communities to validate a sales pitch without transparency

### Best Reddit Use

Use Reddit to learn:

- what people distrust
- what language feels fake
- what gifts people actually recommend
- what "family crest" misconceptions exist
- what genealogy users reject
- what family identity questions are emotionally real

Reddit should improve MyKinLegacy's truthfulness.

## 7. Email Architecture

Email should deepen the relationship after permission.

It should not become generic marketing noise.

### When To Collect Email

Collect email when:

- user starts an interview
- user saves a family motto or symbol result
- user wants a gift idea sent later
- user is waiting for collection delivery
- user wants seasonal reminders
- user wants family memory prompts
- user joins a launch or early access list

### When To Send Email

Send email for:

- interview continuation
- collection progress
- collection ready
- gift deadline reminders
- memory prompts
- seasonal family moments
- anniversary or annual legacy update
- useful guides connected to user intent

### When Not To Disturb

Do not send:

- aggressive discount sequences
- grief-triggered upsells
- generic weekly newsletters without purpose
- repeated "finish checkout" pressure
- public sharing pressure
- irrelevant product expansion emails

### Email Principle

Every email should either:

- help the customer complete meaning
- help preserve memory
- help deliver a gift
- help return at the right life moment

## 8. Referral Architecture

Referral should be natural family sharing.

It should not start as a coupon engine.

### Natural Sharing Types

| Sharing type | Trigger | Growth potential |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Family sharing | "Everyone needs to see this" | High |
| Gift sharing | Recipient reacts emotionally | High |
| Wedding sharing | Guests see symbolic identity | High |
| Reunion sharing | Extended family sees shared artifact | High |
| Memorial sharing | Relatives want remembrance copies | Sensitive but meaningful |
| Parent gift sharing | Siblings coordinate or react | High |
| Grandparent sharing | Children/grandchildren ask for versions | High |

### Referral Flow

Customer creates collection.

Customer shares safe preview with family.

Family member reacts.

Family member asks what it is.

Family member creates a collection for their own life moment.

### Referral Rule

Do not bribe people to share private family meaning.

Make the collection worthy of being shared.

## 9. Traffic Flywheel

The long-term flywheel:

Google -> AI -> Pinterest -> Brand -> Direct -> Repeat -> Sharing -> Google

### How It Works

Google captures explicit intent.

AI Search summarizes and recommends trusted explanations.

Pinterest turns meaning into visual desire.

Brand memory grows when users recognize MyKinLegacy as the family identity collection brand.

Direct traffic grows as people remember the name.

Repeat traffic grows as life moments recur.

Sharing creates new family-network discovery.

More branded searches and mentions strengthen Google and AI visibility.

### Flywheel Health Signals

| Signal | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Branded search grows | Brand memory is forming |
| Family share traffic appears | Collection is worth sharing |
| AI mentions category language | Explanations are citation-ready |
| Pinterest saves become clicks | Visual desire is moving toward action |
| Returning users buy new moments | Lifetime journey is working |
| Direct traffic increases | Brand is becoming remembered |

## 10. Founder Review

The largest future traffic source for MyKinLegacy is likely not one channel.

It will be family-network sharing amplified by search and AI.

Why?

Because MyKinLegacy is not a daily-use tool. It appears at meaningful life moments. Those moments are often social:

- weddings
- anniversaries
- Father's Day
- Mother's Day
- family reunions
- memorials
- new children
- grandparent stories
- family gifts

The first discovery may come from Google, Pinterest, or AI.

But the most defensible growth comes when one family member sees another family member feel recognized.

The founder answer:

The biggest traffic source should become Direct + Family Sharing.

Google and AI will help people discover MyKinLegacy.

Pinterest will help them imagine it.

But family sharing will make them believe it.

If MyKinLegacy becomes the brand families remember when life changes, direct traffic will eventually become the clearest sign that the traffic architecture is working.
