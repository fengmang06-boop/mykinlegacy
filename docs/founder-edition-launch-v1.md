No physical product or shipping is included.

## Delivery Promise

Founder Edition collections are normally reviewed and delivered within two business days after
payment. If clarification or technical recovery is required, support contacts the buyer or provides
a status update. The Founder must release each completed collection using the controlled approval
operation; generation alone does not send the Vault email.

## Corrections And Refunds

Support reviews incorrect recipient or occasion details, missing files, broken files, and delivery
defects. Subjective redesign requests are reviewed individually and do not include unlimited
revisions. Cancellation and refund eligibility follow the published Refund Policy. Support contact:
support@mykinlegacy.com.

## Order Procedure

1. Confirm checkout is enabled and remaining slots are above zero.
2. Monitor the paid order until artifacts and download binaries pass verification.
3. Review the Final Crest and all three publications.
4. Release delivery with `approve_founder_delivery` only after the package passes.
5. Confirm Resend status, Vault open, and Complete Collection download.
6. Record feedback, refund requests, and P0 issues in the Founder operations board.

Any payment, wrong-recipient, broken-file, Vault, ZIP, or email failure is P0 and pauses checkout
until contained. Weak personalization or visual disappointment is P1 and is handled per order
without silently changing the product promise.
