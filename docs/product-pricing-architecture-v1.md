# MyKinLegacy Product & Pricing Architecture v1.0

Status: Strategic commercial model  
Primary brand: MyKinLegacy  
Product anchor: Family Legacy Collection  
Principle: Price the emotional completeness of the collection, not the raw cost of generated files.

This document defines the commercial architecture for MyKinLegacy. It answers what we sell, why customers pay, what remains free, how products ladder upward, and how future revenue can expand without damaging trust.

## 1. What We Really Sell

MyKinLegacy does not primarily sell images, files, prompts, or downloads.

MyKinLegacy sells a private family legacy artifact: a complete, emotionally meaningful, AI-assisted symbolic collection that helps a customer express family identity, preserve memory, or give a personal gift.

The real value is the transformation:

- From scattered family details to a coherent symbolic identity
- From "I need a meaningful gift" to "I have something personal to give"
- From vague heritage curiosity to a private collection with story, symbols, and artifacts
- From a single image idea to a complete keepsake package

The customer is paying for meaning, structure, care, privacy, and a finished collection.

## 2. Why Customers Are Willing to Pay

Customers pay when the product solves an emotional problem better than a generic alternative.

Primary willingness-to-pay drivers:

- Personalization: the output reflects their surname, heritage, values, colors, symbols, motto, and family context.
- Emotional weight: it can be given, framed, kept, or shared with family.
- Completion: the product is a collection, not a loose generated image.
- Interpretation: the customer receives explanations and story, not only visuals.
- Convenience: the guided process turns uncertain family ideas into usable artifacts.
- Privacy: family details are handled as private by default.
- Trust: clear disclaimers reduce fear of fake historical or legal claims.
- Giftability: the collection feels more thoughtful than ordinary digital gifts.

The buyer is not paying because AI exists. The buyer is paying because MyKinLegacy helps them create something their family can keep.

## 3. Free Layer

The free layer should create trust, help users clarify family inputs, and naturally lead toward a paid collection.

Free layer purpose:

- Capture early demand
- Educate users
- Improve paid conversion quality
- Establish MyKinLegacy as the trusted guide
- Avoid commoditizing the core collection

Recommended free tools:

| Free tool | Primary demand | Output | Paid bridge |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Family Motto Generator | Identity, Legacy | Motto options | Add motto to collection |
| Family Symbol Finder | Meaning, Identity | Symbol suggestions | Generate symbolic crest and explanation |
| Family Values Builder | Identity, Legacy | Values statement | Use values in story and certificate |
| Guardian Animal Finder | Meaning, Pride | Animal match | Add to crest variants |
| Heritage Gift Finder | Gift | Gift recommendation | Recommend collection package |
| House Name Generator | Identity | House name ideas | Render in certificate and story |
| Family Story Starter | Memory | Story outline | Upgrade to full story PDF |
| Legacy Letter Prompt Generator | Memory, Legacy | Letter prompts | Add legacy letter pack |

Free layer rules:

- Free tools may provide ideas, frameworks, and previews.
- Free tools should not deliver the complete paid asset package.
- Free tools must include the same trust boundaries as paid products.
- Free tools should collect enough context to make the paid upgrade feel natural, not forced.

## 5. Core Product

The core product is Family Legacy Collection.

Core promise:

A private digital package with symbolic crest artwork, PDF explanations, a heritage certificate, family story, transparent artwork, and secure collection access.

Recommended core price range:

- Standard digital package: $39-$79
- Launch/testing range: $29-$59
- Mature range after proof and stronger examples: $59-$99

Core deliverable logic:

- Multiple symbolic crest variants
- Transparent crest artifact
- Symbol explanation PDF
- Heritage certificate PDF
- Family story PDF
- Download package
- Private vault delivery

The core product should remain the central reference point for all future product extensions.

## 7. Future Subscription

Subscription should not be forced into the MVP. It becomes viable when MyKinLegacy evolves from one-time collection delivery into an ongoing family identity and archive system.

Future subscription concept:

MyKinLegacy Vault Membership

Possible price range:

- $6-$12 per month
- $49-$99 per year

Subscription value:

- Private family vault
- Ongoing artifact storage
- Additional annual legacy prompts
- Seasonal family keepsake drops
- Family story timeline
- New artifact credits
- Multi-member family collaboration
- Anniversary and memorial reminders
- Secure sharing controls

Subscription should only launch when recurring value is real. Do not charge for storage alone unless the archive experience is meaningful.

## 8. Future Physical Products

Physical products should extend successful digital artifacts, not distract from the core.

Future physical product categories:

- Framed wall art
- Printed certificate sets
- Family story books
- Wax seals
- Signet ring previews and later physical rings
- Family banners
- Reunion merchandise
- Archival gift boxes
- Wedding stationery

Physical product rules:

- Validate digital demand first.
- Avoid operational complexity before the core digital business is stable.
- Preserve disclaimers on physical artifacts where appropriate.
- Use premium print and fulfillment partners only when quality is controlled.

Physical products can raise average order value and gift satisfaction, but they also introduce fulfillment, returns, and quality risk.

## 9. Pricing Philosophy

Pricing should reflect emotional completeness, not file count.

Principles:

- Do not race to the bottom against generic AI generators.
- Do not price like a template shop.
- Do not use fake discounts as the main conversion tool.
- Keep entry products accessible, core products meaningful, and premium products clearly richer.
- Price by use case, emotional stakes, and deliverable depth.
- Use transparent package language.
- Avoid making customers feel punished for wanting privacy or basic delivery.

Price should tell the customer: this is thoughtful, complete, and worth keeping.

| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free | Tools, quizzes, prompts | $0 | Demand capture and trust |
| Entry | Small packs | $9-$39 | First purchase |
| Core | Family Legacy Collection | $39-$99 | Main revenue driver |
| Premium | Event or memory collections | $99-$299 | Higher emotional stakes |
| Deep premium | Books, memorial, multi-member projects | $249-$599 | High-touch value |
| Subscription | Vault membership | $49-$99/year | Recurring relationship |
| Physical | Print and heirloom extensions | $39-$499+ | AOV expansion |

The ladder should let customers enter gently, then upgrade when the emotional use case deepens.

## 11. Lifetime Customer Value

MyKinLegacy should think in family life moments, not one transaction.

Potential LTV journey:

1. Free tool: Family Motto Generator
2. Core purchase: Family Legacy Collection
3. Add-on: Wall Art or Certificate Pack
4. Seasonal purchase: Father's Day or Christmas legacy gift
5. Event purchase: Wedding Heritage or Reunion Kit
6. Memory purchase: Grandparent Memory Collection
7. Subscription: private vault and annual legacy prompts
8. Physical product: framed print, book, wax seal, or ring preview

LTV grows when MyKinLegacy becomes the brand customers return to whenever family meaning needs to become an artifact.

## 12. Upgrade Path

Upgrade paths should be tied to human demand, not generic upsells.

Primary upgrade paths:

- Free motto output -> Family Legacy Collection
- Family Legacy Collection -> Collection Plus
- Collection -> Wall Art
- Collection -> Certificate Pack
- Collection -> Social Kit
- Collection -> Wedding Heritage Collection
- Collection -> Grandparent Memory Collection
- Collection -> Vault Membership
- Gift purchase -> future occasion reminder
- Story starter -> Family Story Book

Upgrade language should feel helpful:

- "Complete the collection"
- "Add a printable keepsake"
- "Create a gift-ready version"
- "Preserve this in your family vault"

Avoid manipulative scarcity or shame-based upgrade pressure.

## 13. Bundling Strategy

Bundles should clarify value and reduce decision friction.

Bundle types:

| Bundle | Includes | Use case |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Starter Bundle | Motto, symbols, certificate | Low-cost gift or test purchase |
| Complete Collection | Core product assets | Main offer |
| Gift Bundle | Core collection, gift message, certificate variants | Parent or holiday gift |
| Wedding Bundle | Couple crest, certificate, stationery/social assets | Wedding planning |
| Memory Bundle | Story, certificate, remembrance symbols | Grandparent or memorial |
| Print-Ready Bundle | Artwork, poster PDF, certificate PDF | Framing and home display |
| Family Event Bundle | Crest, banner, badges, social kit | Reunion or celebration |

Bundling should never hide what is included. Customers should understand exactly what they will receive.

## 16. Strategic Conclusion

MyKinLegacy should monetize completeness, trust, and emotional usefulness.

The first commercial job is to make Family Legacy Collection feel worth paying for. The second job is to build entry points and upgrades around real life moments. The third job is to expand into a family identity ecosystem without becoming a generic digital product marketplace.

The business model should remain anchored in one truth:

Customers pay when MyKinLegacy helps them create something their family would be proud to keep.
