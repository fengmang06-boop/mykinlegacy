## 1. What Is a Life Moment?

A Life Moment is a specific change, transition, realization, celebration, loss, or responsibility that makes a person suddenly care more about family identity, memory, belonging, legacy, or meaningful gifting.

Demographics describe who someone is from the outside. Life Moments explain why someone cares right now.

Age, gender, income, and country can help describe context, but they rarely explain the buying trigger. A 28-year-old, a 45-year-old, and a 70-year-old may all buy from MyKinLegacy for the same reason: a parent is aging, a wedding is coming, a family name feels important, or a child just asked where the family came from.

Life Moments matter more because they create:

- Urgency: something changed, and the person wants to act.
- Emotional intensity: the decision is connected to love, grief, pride, belonging, or responsibility.
- Search behavior: the person starts asking questions.
- Gift intent: the person wants to give something meaningful.
- Identity need: the person wants to name or express what the moment means.
- Product readiness: the person can imagine using or sharing a finished collection.

MyKinLegacy should not ask only "Who is the customer?" It should ask:

What just happened in this person's life?

MyKinLegacy 是一个在人生最重要时刻出现的品牌。

Life changed. Family meaning became urgent. MyKinLegacy helps turn that moment into a private collection worth keeping.
