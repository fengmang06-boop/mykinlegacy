# EXP-001: Life Moment Validation

## Experiment ID

EXP-001

## Hypothesis

Adding Life Moment will noticeably improve Family Recognition compared with a normal prompt that only uses surname, country, symbols, colors, and style.

## Variables

| Type | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Control | Prompt-first artifact without explicit life moment |
| Test | Same family inputs plus Life Moment |
| Fixed | Surname, heritage country, preferred animal, colors, style |
| Changed | Life Moment and emotional context |

## Control

Normal prompt format:

"Create a heritage-inspired symbolic family crest for [surname], inspired by [country], using [animal], [colors], and [style]."

## Method

Ten family scenarios were created. Each received:

- A: Prompt-first artifact without Life Moment
- B: Life Moment-first interpretation artifact

No images were generated. The experiment evaluated identity direction, narrative potential, art direction specificity, and recognition likelihood.

## Family Trials

| Family | Control A observation | Test B observation | Control score | Test score |
| --- | --- | --- | ---: | ---: |
| FAM-001 Father passed away | Generic crest for surname with eagle and navy; no grief context | Memorial direction centered on father as protector, restrained tone, legacy certificate | 5.0 | 8.2 |

| FAM-010 New grandparent | Generic legacy crest | Grandparent-to-grandchild continuity, future values, letter artifact | 5.5 | 7.9 |

## Evaluation

| Metric | Control Avg | Test Avg | Observation |
| --- | ---: | ---: | --- |
| Family Recognition | 5.1 | 7.6 | Life Moment made the artifact feel for a specific situation |
| Meaning Consistency | 5.3 | 7.4 | Symbols gained purpose |
| Narrative Consistency | 4.9 | 7.8 | Life Moment created story direction |
| Gift Worthiness | 5.5 | 7.7 | Gift context improved recipient intent |
| Emotional Resonance | 4.8 | 8.0 | Strongest lift |
| Keepsake Value | 5.4 | 7.5 | More likely to be preserved |
| Authenticity | 5.2 | 7.3 | Less generic |
| Story Depth | 4.7 | 7.4 | Story moved beyond surface inputs |
| Identity Strength | 5.6 | 7.3 | Identity became less surname-dependent |
| This Feels Like Us | 5.1 | 7.6 | Recognition improved across all 10 families |

## Expected Result

Life Moment should increase emotional accuracy and recognition.

## Actual Observation

The hypothesis was supported in all 10 internal artifact comparisons. The largest lift appeared in Emotional Resonance and Narrative Consistency. The control artifacts were not necessarily bad; they were simply generic. The test artifacts had a reason to exist.

## What We Learned

Life Moment acts as the interpretation anchor. Without it, the same inputs produce decorative family symbols. With it, symbols start serving a human purpose.

## Should Continue?

Yes.

## Confidence

Medium. This is not yet human-validated, but the signal is consistent across scenarios.
